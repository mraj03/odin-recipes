Cappuccino

Desription

The Italian coffee-based dessert is made with just three ingredients â€” espresso, gelato, and a shot of liqueur. Here's how to make it at home without an espresso machine.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 2 scoops vanilla gelato or ice cream
  • 1 splash amaretto
  • Shaved dark chocolate, for serving

Steps to prepare

  1. Brew the espresso. Using your desired brewing method, brew 1 shot espresso.
  2. Scoop the ice cream. As the espresso is brewing, drop 2 scoops vanilla gelato or ice cream into a small drinking glass.
  3. Add the espresso and amaretto. Pour the espresso shot and 1 splash amaretto over the ice cream.
  4. Garnish and serve. Top with shaved dark chocolate and serve immediately.
